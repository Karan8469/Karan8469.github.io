Three Key Thread Forms

Square Thread

Highest efficiency. Perpendicular flanks create no radial pressure. Ideal for power transmission but difficult to manufacture.

|||||

Acme Thread

Trapezoidal shape (29Â° angle). Easier to manufacture and allows for split nuts to compensate for wear. A common compromise.

/\/\/\

Buttress Thread

Asymmetrical. Handles very high loads in one direction. Used in large vises and presses where force is unidirectional.
